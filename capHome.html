Home

This holiday season is like no other. Gift-giving will be more difficult and families won't be able to come together. This website is a guide for anyone looking for information on having a safe holiday season. Look below to see what each page includes:

  • COVID-19 Safety

Looking for information on COVID-19 in Vancouver? See this page for information on how COVID-19 is spread and what you can do to stop it. Also, learn about restrictions in Vancouver.

  • Holiday Activites

There are some activities that are more high-risk than others. Visit this page to see what you can do to have fun but stay healthy. You can still have a festive holiday season!

  • Gifting Safely

Giving gifts is one of the best part of the holiday season. However, giving presents to people outside your household will be different this year. If you're looking for a guide on holiday gifts during this pandemic, click on the button above!

  • Giving Back

One of the best gifts for yourself and your loved ones is giving back. Whether you're wanting to help your community, donate to a charity or a toy drive, or buy from companies that give back, this page will help you do that.

  • Sources

This page contains all of the sources used for the content included on this website. If you're looking for more information, look no further!
