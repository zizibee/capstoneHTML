Gifting Safely

This page includes some ideas for gifting during the pandemic. Scroll down to see how you can show your appreciation for your loved ones!

Delivered Gifts:

Getting a gift for a loved one delivered to their residence is a great way to show your appreciation while staying more than 6-feet apart. If you can't ship it right to their door, you could also bring their gift and leave it outside to minimize contact.

Online Subscriptions:

Online subscriptions are good if your gift recepient lives far away or wants something you can't deliver. For book lovers, audiobook and e-book subscriptions are a fantastic choice. Streaming services and online classes are also great for a multitude of people.

Homemade Gifts:

Homemade gifts like cards, crafts, and treats show that you took time and effort to make your gift special and personalized. You can mail cards and crafts or send them as pictures digitally. If you decide to make food, ask the recepient if they feel comfortable receiving the gift and make sure to be hygienic during food preparation.

Sustainable Gifts:

Gifting sustainably is easy and saves money while helping the Earth. See three strategies below on how to be sustainable while spreading holiday spirit.

Recycle and Revamp

Repurpose something and refurbish it.

Thrift

Find second-hand items that are more unique.

Eco-friendly Products

Buy products that are sustainable, organic, or able to be used for a long time.
